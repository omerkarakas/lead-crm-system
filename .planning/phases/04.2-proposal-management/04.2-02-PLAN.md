<objective>
Implement proposal generation from templates with variable substitution, unique link generation, and WhatsApp sending functionality.

- WhatsApp integration for sending proposal links
- Public proposal viewing page with accept/reject functionality
- Proposal dialog component for template selection and sending
- Teklif tab on lead detail page and appointment detail modal
</objective>
