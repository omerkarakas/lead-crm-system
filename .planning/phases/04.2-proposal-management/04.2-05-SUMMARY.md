## Decisions Made

1. **Appointment completion does NOT update lead status** - Per plan specification, only proposal responses should affect lead status. Appointments can be completed without changing the lead's status.
2. **Auto-update tracking with explicit fields** - Instead of inferring from offer_response field, use explicit boolean flag and timestamp for clarity and easier querying.
3. **Admin-only override capability** - Only admins can override auto-updated statuses, and they must explicitly check a force checkbox to prevent accidental changes.
4. **Single source of truth for sales phones** - Eliminated broken fallback chain by using ONLY app_settings. No users.phone dependency, no SALES_WHATSAPP_NUMBER env var.
